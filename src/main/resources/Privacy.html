Course Scheduler Privacy Policy

Updated 9/13/2014

  1. What Information We Collect, How We Disclose It, and Your Options

    1. Course Scheduler

      1. Certain versions of Course Scheduler collect anonymous usage data. We make every effort to collect only data that cannot be tied to a named individual. The Keen IO service is used to store this data. To learn more about how Keen IO respects the privacy of the data, you can visit https://keen.io/privacy-policy.
      2. Anonymous information collected by Course Scheduler will be used to improve the application as well as to provide aggregated analytic usage data to our customers.
      3. If your version of the Course Scheduler collects anonymous data, you can opt out of collection in the application settings.

    2. If You Contact Us

      1. If you contact us regarding any of our websites or applications, we may request personal information to better resolve your query.
      2. Any personal data collected if you contact us will be used solely by members of the Course Scheduler team.

  2. Changes to this Policy

    1. We reserve the right to change this policy at any time, however we will alert users to changes within Course Scheduler.

  3. Contact

    1. Please let us know of any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this privacy policy by emailing privacy@coursescheduler.io.
